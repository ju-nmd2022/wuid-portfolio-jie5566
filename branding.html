THE VISUAL IDENTITY GUIDE OF NORTINT DRY GIN

Nortint started the first gin disilery in the north of Sweden from 2016. The gin is richly flavored by juniper berries from nordern Swedish pure forests. The organic botanical ingredients give a strong citrus scent and a pine-like taste, accompanied by a kick of mild bitterness. Lars Svenson and Johan Biger are the master distillers of Nortint dry Gin. They started their friendship when they were kids. They developed their common interests on gin distilleration during the years. Gradually, Nortint dry gin be- comes one of the most popular product in local gin market.

Nortint dry gin is an organic handcrafted product, which is free from additives and artificial flavoring. It is seen as particularly appealing for health-oriented and sustainability aware people. With the exellent quality and childhood friends passion of distillation, Nortint gry gin is expected to be a star among gin products in Systembolaget

BRANDING CONCEPT

The logo design was based on the growth enviorment of Norden Swedish juniper berry. The main firgure of the logo is a simplified lining pine tree, the three short lines and the circle repersent the shape of the juniper berry.
